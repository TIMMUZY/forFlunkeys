  Who can use Homemeal?

The Homemeal vision? A good meal always brings people closer!

We welcome a community of aficcionados, people willing to go toward the culture of sharing. But we mostly meet people coming from various backgrounds: food or gastronomy lovers, rockers and geeks, students and retired people, vegans and meat lovers, wine tasters, stuntmen or magicians... etc…

  Comment rejoindre Homemeal?

To be part of the adventure, just click on the "Sign-in" button, at the top of our homepage and fill-in your email address. You will receive a confirmation email. Then just click on the link within this email and let's go: fill your personal details in less than 2 minutes and be part of a unique experience!

If you wish to fully live a true Homemeal experience, it is possible! You can become a host or just a user experiencing other people meals and services during your trips or in your home city in order to meet new people.

  Do I have to confirm my email address and phone number?

Your personal information are essential in order to contact a host or to be part of a meal. It is therefore important to duly complete your Homemeal profile.

It is also important for us to be able to send you updates and remain in contact with you. If your personal information are incomplete, you will not be able to be a user or a host.

Users are more likely to trust a host with a lot of information and details. Host are also more willing to accept a request from a user if this one shows a secured profile.

  How can I edit my profile pic?

Your profile pic is an important element in order to create a good trusting user/host relationship. When you are connected on your account, you just have to go on the "My account" page: from here, you will be able to download a profile picture by clicking on "Pick a file" and then "Modify". The bees knees of the thing would be to pick a recent picture with a high quality!

  What shall I do if I had lost my password?

If you cannot remember your password, you can easily reinitialise it by doing so: on the homepage, click on "Sign-in", like when you wish to connect yourself to your account. From here, just click on "I forgot my password", then fill your email address and you will receive a link in your mail box in order to create a new password.

  I want to change my password, how can I?

If you can remember your password but still want to change it, connect yourself on your personal account and go on the "Settings" tab then on "Security". You will just have to fill in the blank fields the password you would like to have. On the very same page will appear a confirmation notice. You will also receive an email confirming your action.

  How to change the email address that I'm using for Homemeal?

In order to change your email address, go to "My account". Replace the field corresponding to your old email address by the new one and click on "update" at the bottom of the page.

  How can change my password if I'm connected via Facebook?

When you sign-in on Homemeal with Facebook, your email address and password are automatically given to us in order for you to fully access our Homemeal website. Your Facebook account and your Homemeal account are then paired up and you will be able to automatically connect on Homemeal. Nevertheless, you won't be able to connect without going by Facebook because you would not have been able to put a password on your Homemeal account.

Then you can connect via Facebook to your Homemeal account and go onto the "Settings" tab, then "Security" in order to indicate a password that will be the one for your Homemeal account.

If you do not have to opportunity to connect via Facebook, then execute the same steps than those when you have lost your password in order to create a new one.

  Any other question on this topic?

If you have any other question or comment about this topic, please send us a mail on the contact form or write us an email at contact@homemeal.com. We will answer you on a short notice.
