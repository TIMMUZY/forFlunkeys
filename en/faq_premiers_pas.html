  Why Homemeal?

Nowadays, we have the chance to travel with more ease than ever before in all the great cities of our world. That being said, a big problem remains, it is that travellers don't really have the chance to communicate and exchange with the locals yet representing the true spirit of their city.

It is this paradoxe that has motivated our founders to create Homemeal: travellers won't be considered anymore as simple tourists.

They are able to discover a culture in its purest form, live an outstanding experience around the first social link, the one that goes within the heart of the notion of sharing: the dinning table!

  How does it work?

Homemeal made the meeting between inhabitants and travelers the simplest possible.

If you are a traveler and you want to taste the meal of a host, you just have to sign-in on our website then discover the various meals offered by Homemeal and its hosts and choose the one that you want. You will then just have to make a reservation online to share a wonderful moment with your host!

If you want to receive people over, become a Homemeal host and access your personalised space in order to create meals: add pictures, descriptions, indicate your suggestions and the date and time you're available. Once your meal is posted, you can discuss with your guests, handle and access your reservations and then go cooking!

Leave then place to discovery and pleasure.

Learn more by clicking on the following link: https://www.homemeal.com/en/howitworks

  How much does Homemeal use cost?

Good news: the best things in life are free. Sign-in on Homemeal and offer your services is totally free!

If you wish to welcome people at your table, all you will need is a little time, some imagination and motivation to put online your most wonderful meals after you signed-in.

If you'd rather get a table at one of our host place, you will be able to find the perfect meal for you and make a reservation in a few clicks in order to live a great Homemeal experience!

  Is Homemeal uniquely reserved for travelers?

Nope! You just have to be open to other cultures or want to meet different people. If you wish, you can even go have a dinner at your neighbour's place!

  Can I make a reservation even though I follow a specific diet?

Of course you can! Homemeal is a community open to everybody, and we are doing our best in order to offer you the best experiences possible, wether you are intolerant to some type of food or following a specific diet. On your personal profile page, we advise you to indicate your diet restrictions.

As a guest, you can also choose the meals that suit you by using filters in your research.

  When will I receive my host information following my reservation?>

At Homemeal, we like to do things right, and speed is one of our key-word: you will automatically receive the personal information of your host at the very same time this one will have accepted your reservation.

We leave our host a 48 hours delay in order to answer your request, passed this, your reservation will unfortunately be cancelled.

Do not forget that you do not have the right to communicate personal information within the Homemeal internal messenger tool.

  Any other question on this topic?

If you have any other question or comment about this topic, please send us a mail on the contact form or write us an email at contact@homemeal.com. We will answer you on a short notice.
