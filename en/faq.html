  What is Homemeal?

Homemeal is an Internet platform that allows an amateur host to receive guests on reservation from all around the world! Live unforgettable moments of sharing and exchanges around a unique culinary experience.

  Is sign in on Homemeal for free?

Yes, sign in on Homemeal is entirely free. Once you signed in, you will have the opportunity to access complementary services and you will be guided at every steps of the process.

For the Host: If you wish to show your talents as a Chef and display your secret home recipes, Homemeal is your best friend! You will have the opportunity to create a publication very quickly and receive demands from potential guests coming from everywhere in the world.

For the Guests: If you want to go and meet people, you will just have to enter the city of your choice and see the different meals available in the area.

  Does Homemeal take fees on the service that I provide when receiving guests?

As a host, you are free to choose the amount you want to receive when organising an event. This will include the price of the groceries you are going to use but also the price of your hospitality! Homemeal adds up a commission fee of around 15% (rounded up values) over the price that you have chosen. Meaning that you will receive the integrality of the amount you asked to be paid for the service you will provide. The guest will take care of paying the commission fees to Homemeal.

  How can I report the revenues that I get from organising events on Homemeal?

As a Homemeal host, your activity is considered as "co-cooking" (meaning that you cook and share meals in your home with guests).

In France, revenues generated by this kind of activity do not need to be reported.

For more information about this topic, please check the following circular of the Ministry of the Economy and Finance about this topic.

http://bofip.impots.gouv.fr/bofip/1322-PGP.html
  Do I have to adopt any kind of legal status to be a host with Homemeal?

Keep in mind that Homemeal is more about the human adventure that it offers: being open-minded about meeting people from all over the world in order to exchange and share! Live great moments with ephemeral friends. Homemeal is about moments with friends, not a commercial activity.

Thus, when you organise Homemeal events in your home, they are to be considered as private: it means that you welcome people at your place from times to times and that is not your principal source of revenue.

You have to apply the French legislation or the one running in your country. In France, revenues from "co-cooking" activities are not to be reported.

If you host people as a profession and you receive people with Homemeal, you will of course have to apply all the legal rules that are applicable to your activity, in terms of accounting, insurance, financial guarantee and safety.

  Does Homemeal take a commission fee when I make a reservation with a host?

If you wish to make a reservation with a Homemeal host, you will have to pay the amount freely fixed by the host as well as a commission fee at the time of the payment of your reservation.

Did you say "Why"? At Homemeal, we are happy to make the link between people from all around the world and local host in order to live special culinary experiences in a safe way and with all the serenity possible. Going with Homemeal also implies to have an insurance if any problem occurs as a host but also as a guest under the amount of 300 000 euros. We have to finance it by taking a commission fee of around 15%.

To illustrate this, let's say that you make a reservation for a typical Alsatian brunch worthing 25€, you will pay the amount of 28,75€ (rounded up to 29€)

  When will I receive the personal information of my host following my reservation?

At Homemeal, we do things well, and speed is one of our keyword: you will automatically receive the personal information related to your host at the very same time he will have confirmed your reservation.

We ask our hosts to answer you in less than 48 hours, passed this delay, your reservation will unfortunately be cancelled.

Don't forget that you are not allowed to communicate personal information over the private messaging service provided by Homemeal.
