  How can I know if a host is a good cook?

There are many ways to evaluate the quality of a host. First of all, you are invited to have a look over the pictures that have been posted by the host on our website.

We also recommend that you read the comments left by other Homemeal guests on our website.

Furthermore, you can also communicate with the host on our website to have more information.

  How much time do I have to answer?

We ask our host to answer you under 24 hours but past this, your reservation will be cancelled.

  How much time will it take for the host to confirm my reservation?

We encourage the communication between hosts and guests. You can expect to have a confirmation in the 24 hours following your reservation.

If you do not receive an answer, the reservation will be cancelled.

  How can I get more information on my host?

There is plenty of information available on your host.During the process of inscription of a host, they have to fill up their hobbies and why they want to receive people over…

If you wish to have more information, just contact your host via the private message system embedded on our website.

Also you will be able to see the pictures of your host on his or her profile, and the pictures of his or her home.

Do not forget, you eat with your host, therefore the meals will be prepared with love and carefulness like if you were family.

If you find any trouble with a host, we will refund you and delete the profile of your host of our website.

  When will I receive the additional information concerning my meal and the exact place?

At Homemeal we do things simply and efficiently. At the moment the host confirms the reservation, you will receive automatically, all the details you will need.

If you have made a request for a reservation, you should receive an answer in the 48 hours following it.

Don’t forget that you cannot exchange your personal information in the private messages.

  What happens if I have to cancel my reservation?

We understand that sometimes things come up and you have to cancel your reservation on Homemeal.

To facilitate that process we have created rules concerning that matter:

You cannot be refunded when you cancel a reservation

If you wish to cancel your reservation, please send us an email at contact@homemeal.com

  Will I be refunded if the host cancels my meal?

Yes you will. We will either reimburse you, or if you wish, do our best to find you another table.

  Does Homemeal takes fees out of my meal?

Homemeal adds a commission fee of 15% which adds up to the original price of the meal, meaning that the guest only will pay for those fees.

  Any other question?

If you have any other question or comment, please send us a mail on the contact form or write us an email at contact@homemeal.com. We will answer you on a short notice.
